About Ocean Alive

We are a global community dedicated to protecting and restoring the world’s oceans.

Our Mission

Ocean Alive is committed to safeguarding marine life, fighting plastic pollution, and preserving coral reefs.

We work with scientists, volunteers, and environmental organizations across the world to restore ocean health.

Our goal is to ensure that the ocean — the source of life — remains alive for future generations.
